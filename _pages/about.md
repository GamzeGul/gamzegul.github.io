I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Electrical and Computer Engineering at Northwestern University, working with Prof. Prem Kumar.

I received my Ph.D. in Applied Physics from Northwestern University in 2026. I earned my M.S. degree in Physics from Koç University and B.S. degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Bilkent University.

My research focuses on quantum networks, nonlinear optics, and quantum information processing.
